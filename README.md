## What this is:
Analysis of NCAA Men's Basketball Tournament scores (1985 - 2025) to evaluate square outcomes distributions.

## Key Insight:
Though assignment of square is random, certain squares occur significantly more often than others - up to ~4x difference.

## Why it matters:
Challenges the assumption that square pools are purely based on luck.
